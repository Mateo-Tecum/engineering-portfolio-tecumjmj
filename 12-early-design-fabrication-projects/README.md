# Early Design and Fabrication Projects

## Overview

This folder contains early engineering design and fabrication projects that introduced me to CAD, prototyping, circuits, 3D printing, laser cutting, and technical communication.

These projects helped build the foundation for later work in mechatronics, instrumentation, research tooling, and mechanical design.

## Included Projects

### CAD and 3D-Printed Phone Case

Designed a custom phone case using CAD and fabricated it with 3D printing.

### PVC Trombone Prototype

Built a functional trombone-inspired instrument using PVC pipe and a custom mouthpiece. The prototype was able to produce multiple notes and demonstrated basic acoustic and mechanical design principles.

### Water Bottle Prototypes

Designed multiple CAD water bottle prototypes, including a final custom 3D-printed bottle concept.

### Water Filtration Prototype

Developed a basic water filtration system using materials such as charcoal, rock, sand, and filter paper to explore filtration design and sustainability concepts.

### Basic Circuit and Arduino Work

Completed early projects involving LEDs, resistors, soldering, wiring, and Arduino-based control.
